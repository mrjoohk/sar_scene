SAR Requirements Reviewer (Lite Checklist)

Resolution & Performance
- Cross-range and range resolution are quantified with units.
- Bandwidth, PRF, Doppler, platform velocity conditions are stated when relevant.
- Latency and memory usage limits are explicit (s/image, GB).

Reproducibility
- Scenario and seed are specified for deterministic runs.
- Input shape or dataset reference is included.

Evidence & Citations
- source_citations contain valid KB citation_key values.
- Verification method is test or analysis and is feasible.
